## Design Context

### Users
Indian freelancers and agencies receiving international payments, using the Playto Payout dashboard to monitor incoming funds and initiate payouts to bank accounts.

### Use Cases
1. Select a merchant/business entity.
2. Monitor balances (total, held/in-flight, available) and recent credit activity.
3. Request new payouts by entering an amount and choosing a bank account.
4. Track payout history with statuses (pending, processing, completed, failed) and failure reasons.

### Brand Personality
Premium, trustworthy, and “alive” in a controlled, professional way. For this refinement pass: calmer and quieter (less aggressive text and less saturated accents) while staying bold and confident.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark Ledger: industrial precision with luxury-finance restraint.
- Theme: deep navy-black surfaces with warm gold accents.
- Actions: electric cyan but softened/muted for “quieter” mode.
- Success: green but desaturated and less glowing.
- Failures: crimson/red but toned down.
- Motion: subtle feedback only (quiet entrances; LIVE indication should be present but not distracting).

### Design Principles
1. **Quieter first:** reduce saturation, glow, and letter-spacing; keep hierarchy via spacing and weight, not harsh intensity.
2. **Premium clarity:** data-dense UI with clean contrast; avoid aggressive all-caps + wide tracking everywhere.
3. **Restraint in decoration:** remove or tone down gradients/shines unless they convey state/function.
4. **Subtle motion:** keep functional animations but reduce intensity and distance.
5. **Financial readability:** use monospace for money/IDs; ensure numbers remain crisp and consistently aligned.
